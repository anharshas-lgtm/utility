Delete Your Account

At CampusSell, you have full control over your data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open the CampusSell app
  2. Go to your profile section
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Request Deletion Without App Access

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us using the email below.

Email: anharshas@gmail.com

Information to Include

  • Your registered phone number or email
  • confirmation message or mail from the registered channel is needed for approving the deletion
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal data will be removed from our systems
  • Your listings and uploaded content will be deleted

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited period if required for legal obligations, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 3–5 business days.

Important Note

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you really want to delete your account before proceeding.
