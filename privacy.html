Privacy Policy for CampusSell

Effective Date: 02 April 2026

CampusSell (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the CampusSell mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, Email address, Phone number (used for contact in ads), Profile photo (optional).

Non-Personal Information: Device info (model, OS), App usage data, Location information (if allowed).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable buying, selling, and posting of items
  • To send notifications about new listings or updates
  • To improve the app experience and troubleshoot issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

When you post a listing, your phone number will be visible to other users who want to contact you about the listing. By posting a listing, you consent to sharing your phone number.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only with service providers for hosting, analytics, or legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve performance and analyze app usage. You can disable tracking in your device settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also opt out of notifications. Contact us at your_email@example.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for college students aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the revised Effective Date.
