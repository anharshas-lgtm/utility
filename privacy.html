Privacy Policy for CampusSell

Effective Date: 02 April 2026

CampusSell (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • college details(department,year,programme)
  • Phone number (used for contact in listings)
  • Profile photo and images uploaded in listings

Non-Personal Information:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To allow posting and viewing of listings
  • To enable communication between users
  • To send notifications related to app activity
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

3. Sharing of Information

When you post a listing, your phone number may be visible to other users who wish to contact you. By posting a listing, you consent to sharing your phone number for this purpose.

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data with trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, and notifications
  • Cloudinary: Storage and delivery of images

These services may process your data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data and listings are removed from our systems within a reasonable time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data

You can delete your account directly within the app or contact us at anharshas@gmail.com.

7. Permissions

CampusSell may request the following permissions:

  • Internet: To connect to our services
  • Notifications: To send updates and alerts
  • Storage/Media: To upload and display images in listings

These permissions are used only for core app functionality.

8. Children’s Privacy

CampusSell is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
