FAQ - CampusSell

1. How do I create an account?

You can sign up using your email or phone number. Follow the verification process to activate your account.

2. How do I post an item for sale?

Go to the "Sell" section, upload images, add title, description, and price, then post your listing.

3. How do I buy an item?

Browse listings and contact the seller directly using the phone number provided in the listing.

4. Can I post services as well?

Yes, you can post services like tutoring, repairs, or other campus-related services.

5. How do I edit or delete my posts?

Go to "My Posts", select your listing, and choose edit or delete.

6. Is the app free to use?

Yes, CampusSell is completely free for students.

7. Who can see my posts?

Posts are visible to users within your campus community.

8. Why is my phone number visible?

Your phone number is shared only in listings so that interested users can contact you directly.

9. How do I report a problem?

You can contact support via email or use reporting options inside the app.
