FAQ - Campus Marketplace

1. How do I create an account?

You can create an account using your campus email. Go to the registration page, fill in your details, and verify your email to activate your account.

2. How do I post an item for sale?

Go to the "Sell" section, upload images of your item, add a title, description, and price, and click "Post". Your item will appear in the marketplace immediately.

3. How do I buy an item?

Browse items in the marketplace. Click on the one you like, and you’ll see the seller’s contact or chat option. Arrange the transaction directly with the seller.

4. Can I post services as well?

Yes! You can post services like tutoring, computer repair, or campus delivery. Just choose the "Service" category when posting.

5. How do I edit or delete my posts?

Go to "My Posts", select the post you want to edit or delete, and choose the appropriate option. Changes are reflected immediately.

6. Is the app free to use?

Yes, the Campus Marketplace app is completely free for students to buy, sell, or post services.

7. Who can see my posts?

Posts are visible to all users within the campus community. Only students registered with the campus email can access the marketplace.

8. How do I report a problem or inappropriate post?

You can report any issues by clicking the "Report" button on the post or contacting support via the app’s help section.
