About CampusSell

CampusSell is a simple marketplace designed to connect students within a campus to buy, sell, and share items and services بسهولة.

Our Story

When I joined college, I needed a cycle and some basic items. Even though many seniors and other students had these items available, there was no easy way to connect with them.

This gap made everyday needs harder than they should be. That’s when the idea for CampusSell was born — to create a platform where students can easily connect and help each other by buying, selling, or offering services within the campus.

What We Do

  • Help students buy and sell used items easily
  • Enable sharing of services within campus
  • Connect buyers and sellers directly
  • Promote reuse and affordability

Why CampusSell?

  • Made specifically for campus communities
  • Simple and easy to use
  • Direct communication between users
  • Saves time and money

Our Vision

We aim to make campus life more connected, affordable, and sustainable by helping students make better use of available resources around them.
